The Peersview Online Campus

An Online Community for Students Within an Academic Institution

University life is more interesting when you are part of a community

“What is the Peersview Campus”

The Campus is an enterprise social network designed to integrate purposeful social collaboration into learning. It serves as a virtual Campus for students within an academic institution.

How it works

Peersview creates a vibrant and engaging online campus for students.

Frequently asked questions

If you can’t find the answer to your question, please contact us.

  • Why should my institution subscribe to the Peersview Campus?

    The ability for education providers to create quality student experience is becoming increasing difficult due to varying factors.

    Whilst online delivery enables learning flexibility and reduced costs - it can also result in disconnected and disengaged students.

    Peersview helps institutions adapt by implementing social technology to better connect, and engage students from enrolment to graduation.

  • How much does Peersview Campus Cost?

    Contact us.

  • What is required to setup a Peersview Campus?

    Please contact us for details.

  • When should students start using Peersview Campus?

    We recommend institutions invite students to join their Peersview network from enrolment (or even earlier if possible).

  • What support is available to students?

    Support is available according to Subscriptions selected.

  • What level of control does an institution have over its Peersview Campus?

    Control would be according to Administrative Privileges in subscription.
