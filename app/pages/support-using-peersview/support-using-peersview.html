What is Peersview?

Peersview is a place where geographically disconnected peers can meet through their common interests. Whether you are interested in Sports, Biotech, Fashion, Make up, Music, Philosophy etc. discover, interact and share experiences with people who share these interests with you.

Should I Protect my posts?

What is the difference between public and protected Posts?

  • When you sign up for Peersview, your Posts are public by default; anyone can view and interact with your Posts. Should you choose to protect your posts, you can do so through your account settings.
  • If you protect your Post, you’ll receive a request when new people want to follow you, which you can approve or deny. Accounts that began following you before you protected your Posts will still be able to view and interact with your protected Posts unless you block them.

Who can see my Posts?

  • Public Posts (the default setting) are visible to anyone, whether or not they have a Peersview account.
  • Protected Posts may only be visible to your Peersview followers.

Following & Unfollowing People

When you are following a person, you will receive a notification and/or a recent activity item when a user posts on that page.


Blocking People

Block is a feature that helps you control how you interact with other accounts on Peersview. This feature helps users in restricting specific accounts from contacting them, seeing their Posts, and following them.


How to Become Top rated?

You become a Top-rated member of Peersview.com through your contributions on the platform. The following contributions play a role in your ratings:

  • Number of Contributions to the platform i.e posts and questions
  • Ratings your posts receive from other users.
  • Number of threads you have started
