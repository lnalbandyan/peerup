About Us

How it all started

Our founder, Gabriel Amodu, wanted to create an online place where students studying similar disciplines in transnational geographical proximities can connect to share their academic knowledge and social experiences.

Here at Peersview, we strongly believe that the collaborative and interactive features of a social networking platform can help you and other users obtain knowledge and learn in an enjoyable way.

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect geographically disconnected peers through their interests, with a platform where they can share knowledge and social experiences.
