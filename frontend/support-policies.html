Account security Tips:

To help keep your account secure, we recommend the following best practices:

  • Use a strong password that you don’t reuse on other websites.
  • Use login verification.
  • Be cautious of suspicious links and always make sure you’re on persview.com before you enter your login information.
  • Never give your username and password out to third parties, especially those promising to get you followers, make you money, or verify you.
  • Make sure your computer software, including your browser, is up-to-date with the most recent upgrades and anti-virus software.

The Peersview Rules

We believe that everyone should have the power to create and share knowledge and social experiences with their without barriers. In order to protect the experience and safety of people who use Peersview, there are some limitations on the type of content and behavior that we allow. All users must adhere to the policies set forth in the Peersview Rules. Failure to do so may result in the temporary locking and/or permanent suspension of account(s).

Please note that we may need to change these rules from time to time and reserve the right to do so. The most current version will always be available in the Help center.
